01

Functional Hierarchy

The Bauhaus principle of "form follows function" finds its ultimate expression in the Sistema 01234, where each cognitive level serves a specific functional purpose within the architecture of consciousness. This systematic approach eliminates ornamental thinking in favor of pure cognitive utility.

"Eliminate everything superfluous. Every thought, every process, every element must serve the ultimate function: the clear manifestation of consciousness into reality."
— Bauhaus Cognitive Manifesto

03

Editorial Function

The integration of Bauhaus functional principles with academic editorial rigor creates a communication system where every visual element serves the dual purpose of aesthetic clarity and cognitive function. Typography becomes architecture, layout becomes logic.

This synthesis eliminates the false dichotomy between form and content, creating a unified field where the message and its presentation work as a single, functional system. The geometric elements are not decorative but serve as cognitive anchors, helping readers navigate complex theoretical territories with the same precision that Bauhaus architects used to navigate physical space.

"In the marriage of function and form, consciousness finds its clearest expression. The Sistema 01234 is not theory about design—it is design thinking itself."
— S. Forés, Functional Consciousness Studies
